Jounral Database Slurp


The system is designed to solve the problem of updating the database of potential reviewers for Dialogue: Canadian Philosophical Review. The project is to scrape philosophy department websites from universities in the US, UK, Australia, Canada, and New Zealand for new reviewers not currently in the database, reviewers who are in the database but whose information needs to be updated, and reviewers who are in the database but not found on their expected department website and need to be removed from the database. The system will be able to automatically parse the contents of the pages for the most part. The system is semi automated, where it automaically parses content from pages and requires manual intervention for some profiles.
